# Responsible Play

ScratchScope treats lottery spending as entertainment spending only.

Core guardrails:

- Budget controls are visible.
- Cool-off mode hides purchase suggestions.
- Unchecked tickets are prioritized before new play.
- Sample data is labeled.
- Retailer activity is informational only.
- Historical patterns do not predict future results.
- The app must not use guaranteed-win, lucky-store, due-number, exploit, loophole, or beat-the-system language.
